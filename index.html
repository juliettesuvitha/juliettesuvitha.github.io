Dell XPS 14z Campaign

Background:
The Dell XPS 14z is a streamlined consumer notebook with a design sensibility that represents a game-changing approach to the way Dell develops its consumer mobility product range. With its small form factor and lightweight nature, the product launch demands a campaign that showcases the efficacies of the product in a way never seen before.

Our Solution:
We created a campaign that was stylish yet mature without straying from the master brand sensibilities. We created a 3-phase campaign strategy that aims to develop product interest through social media and leverage on the reach of traditional medias like newsprint/online ads as well as radio ads, which will coalesce to a roadshow in ION Orchard featuring radio personalities Vernetta Lopez d Yasmine Ang (on-air event reporting) and then a final phase of sustained activities to further enforce the product message.

Health Promotion Board

Background:
Although residents in Singapore today enjoy good health with high life expectancy and low mortality rates, we want to further improve our health status and continue to enjoy good health as we age.

To achieve this, HPB assumes the role of the main driver for national health promotion and disease prevention programmes. Our goal is to increase the quality and years of healthy life and prevent illness, disability and premature death.

Our Solution:
Our design choices were made after careful consideration of the target audience of the Stimulate Your Mind toolkit: the elderly in Singapore. We named the kit The Goodness Project, reflecting the overarching concept behind the toolkit, which is to promote mental wellbeing, or ‘goodness’.
